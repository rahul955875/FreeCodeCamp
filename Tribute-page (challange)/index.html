Brendan Eich

"The Architect of the Web"

"JavaScript is the assembly language of the web." - Brendan Eich

Here’s a timeline of Brendan Eich, the creator of JavaScript:

  • 1961:

    Brendan Eich is born on July 4th in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania.

  • 1985:

    Graduates with a bachelor's degree in mathematics and computer science from the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign.

  • 1986:

    Earns a master's degree in computer science from the University of Illinois.

  • 1995:

    Creates JavaScript in just 10 days while working at Netscape Communications.

  • 1998:

    Co-founds the Mozilla project, which aims to create an open-source web browser.

  • 2004:

    Helps launch Mozilla Firefox, a widely popular web browser.

  • 2005:

    Becomes Chief Technology Officer (CTO) at Mozilla Corporation.

  • 2014:

    Briefly serves as CEO of Mozilla Corporation before stepping down.

  • 2015:

    Co-founds Brave Software and creates the Brave browser, focusing on privacy and security.

  • 2021:

    Celebrates 25 years of JavaScript, reflecting on its impact as the most widely used programming language in the world.

  • Present:

    Brendan Eich continues to innovate in the tech industry, particularly in web development and online privacy.

  • "Brendan Eich is the godfather of JavaScript. His contribution to the web is immeasurable, and his influence continues to drive innovation today."
